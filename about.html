About Vache Noire



Vache Noire prides itself on being able to provide and intimate and exclusive experience. We want to supply our guests with fine dining and the utmost relaxation to create an unforgettable memory. Vache Noire was founded in 1934 on the foundations of sustainability and serenity. Our vast selection presents our customers with everything from the softest cake to the hottest latte.






Through the use of solar panels and locally grown produce, we have supported and prioritized the movement for sustainability whenever possible. Our natural scenery, consisting of hand-strung plants and a riverbed, fosters a sense of peace and relaxation. With both outdoor and indoor dining options, serenity is guaranteed.

Our menu was carefully curated to fulfill our guests every desire, with a large assortment of beverages and food from around the globe. We offer nutrient rich meals that never lack in flavor.





Established in 1934, our founders Lourival and Florette Clément were a married couple who deeply admired scenery and food. This admiration would grow to be a life long passion of theirs that would turn into the foundation of Vache Noire. From then on Lourival and Flourette dedicated the rest of their lives to the success and growth of Vache Noire. Today, we can happily say the same. The great-grandchildren of the couple still work hard to maintain the café and treat it with the love and respect that their great-grandparents did.
