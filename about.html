Welcome to Vache Noire!

Vache Noire was created in 1934 on the foundation of on love, kindness, and sustainability.

Our vast selection presents our customers with everything from the softest cake to the hottest latte.


The History of Vache Noire

Established on August 8th, 1934, our founders Jules and Violette Clément were a married couple who have had a heavy passion and love for cafés.

This passion was so great that the two decided to open their own café right in the center of New York City.

From then on Jules and Violette dedicated the rest of their lives to the success and growth of Vache Noire.

Today, we can happily say the same. The children of the couple still work hard to maintain the café and treat it with the love and respect that their parents did.
